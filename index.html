How does First Choice Personal Loans work?

To put it simply: First Choice is a matching service and not a lender. We go to extensive lengths to match you with the best lender to fund your loan. While we work directly with some lenders, we also work with lending networks very similar to ours. These lending networks all contain their own portfolio of direct lenders and lending networks. By doing this we give you the greatest chance of getting approved because not only will your application hit qualified lenders in our network but it will also give you the ability to reach a lender in another network who might be willing to fund your loan. Once you apply we filter your application through criteria from all our lenders and then the matching process begins. All applications are processed in real-time so once you hit the submit button your application is presented to our network of lenders one by one to try and find a lender that matches your request. Most process times take between 2-5 minutes.

Upon a successful match you will be redirected to the lenders loan agreement where you will receive your loan offer. Here you will find the identity of the creditor, the amount financed, the itemization of amount financed, the finance charge, the annual percentage rate, your payment schedule and your total number of payments. Lender terms vary so make sure to read your offer thoroughly to ensure you understand all the key elements of the lending agreement before accepting the lenders offer. Usually the typical APR range is anywhere from 6.59% up to 39.99% APR for most personal loan products offered through the lending partners on this site. Generally, the typical representative APR range is somewhere between 261% and 1404% for a short term loan that is intended for 14 days. If you agree to the lenders terms you may be contacted to verify important information so your money can be deposited directly into your bank account. The amount of time it takes to receive your funds will also vary depending on your lender and how quickly your loan can be approved based on the information you provided them. However, most loans will be funded with 1-5 business day.

If you have any questions or concerns about how our service works please feel free to email us or give us a call and let us know what we can do to help you. We appreciate your business and look forward to being your First Choice when it comes to personal or short-term loans.
